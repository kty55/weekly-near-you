Echo Park Lotus Festival, and yes, Yoshi's invited
2026-07-11 and 2026-07-12 · Echo Park Lake, 751 Echo Park Ave
It's the rare pick this week that's genuinely all three of your loves at once: outdoors, food-and-culture, AND Yoshi comes too. The free lakeside festival is a short hop from 90039, with lotus blooms on the water, dragon-boat races, food and live music, this year honoring Sri Lanka (dates and theme confirmed via LA Rec & Parks). And it clears the trust gate on the strongest evidence there is: the festival's OWN FAQ says, verbatim, 'Yes, leashed pets are welcome throughout the festival.' No confirm-before-you-go asterisk like the markets or the flea. Yoshi actually comes, and it's a big delightful day rather than an every-weekend errand. Bring water for him and stick to the shaded lake-path edges when the crowd thickens.
